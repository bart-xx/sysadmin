Some unscientific benchmarks measuring the effect of jumbo frames and Xen on ethernet throughput.

The benchmark was done with netpipe-tcp. All numbers are in MBit/sec, and are averaged over 3 runs of NPtcp.

MTU No Xen Xen dom0 <-> dom0 domU <-> domU
Without bridging+bonding With bridging+bonding
1500 896.734 Not tested 897.024 895.774
8994 941.445 941.386 941.332 940.485

Used hardware:

  • GbE network
  • Dell blades PE1955
  • Broadcom NetXtreme II BCM5708, PCI-X 64-bit 133MHz
  • 2x Dual Core Intel Xeon 5160, 3.0 GHz
